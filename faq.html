Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We're here to help you understand more about our team, goals, and opportunities.

What does the YB Empire team do?
We are a growth-oriented sales team focused on telemarketing and customer engagement within the Coway network. We provide health and wellness solutions to homes while building future sales leaders.
Do I need prior sales experience to join?
No, prior experience is not strictly required. We provide comprehensive training, weekly coaching, and mentorship to help you develop the skills you need to succeed as a Sales Advisor and beyond.
What is the High Performance Team (HPT)?
The HPT is an elite standard within our team reserved for individuals who consistently demonstrate exceptional sales performance, leadership, and a strong commitment to our core values.
How does the career progression work?
Every member starts with a strong foundation and has the opportunity to progress from Trainee to Sales Advisor, Senior Advisor, and eventually Team Leader through structured guidance and performance milestones.
